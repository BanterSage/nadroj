Early Days

In the early days, Jordan was online often, but not too much, it was a moderate amount. Jordan had a combination of work and university in Brock University that caused him to not have the ability to be online most times






Mid Days

While still being in university, Jordan started making more appearences despite workload being just as high as it previously was. Later Days
